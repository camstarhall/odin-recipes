Caprese Salad

Description

Experience the refreshing simplicity of our Caprese Salad. This classic Italian dish showcases the vibrant flavors of ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves. Drizzled with balsamic glaze and a touch of olive oil, this salad is a delightful and colorful appetizer or side dish.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella cheese into 1/4-inch thick slices.
  2. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a platter, alternating between them. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the slices.
  3. Drizzle the extra virgin olive oil over the salad.
  4. Drizzle the balsamic glaze over the salad in a zigzag pattern.
  5. Season the salad with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the Caprese Salad immediately as a refreshing appetizer or side dish.
