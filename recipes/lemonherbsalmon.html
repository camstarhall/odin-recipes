Grilled Lemon Herb Salmon

Description

Elevate your seafood experience with our Grilled Lemon Herb Salmon. Fresh salmon fillets are marinated in a zesty blend of lemon juice, herbs, and spices, then grilled to perfection. The result is a mouthwatering dish with a flavorful crust and tender, flaky fish that will impress any seafood lover.

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets (6 ounces each)
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Steps:

  1. In a shallow dish, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Add the salmon fillets to the marinade, turning them to coat evenly. Let them marinate for 20-30 minutes at room temperature.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Lightly oil the grates to prevent sticking.
  4. Remove the salmon from the marinade, allowing any excess to drip off. Place the fillets on the grill, skin-side down.
  5. Grill the salmon for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until it easily flakes with a fork and has reached your desired level of doneness.
  6. Transfer the grilled salmon to a serving platter and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh herbs and lemon wedges if desired.
